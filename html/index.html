Who am I?

I would say I'm definately an odd-ball of different era's. Born on January 1st, 1991 I'm both young enough to be considered a Millennial but also old enough to remember the startings of commercial internet, AOL, GameBoy, and DOS. I have always loved technology and it's potential, but before formal schooling I would say I mostly dabbled and played with it. Video games were always an interest to me and the thoughts of how they worked. I remember messing around on our computer when I was 5-ish before the big internet boom learning the gist of the windows directory system before my grandfather could turn the machine on! Ever sience then I've been a tinkerer, always looking for new little things I could do. What I lacked in direct knowledge I made up for in determination and work ethic.

Outside of technology I have always loved the beach and the ocean. My original thought in life was that I would become a marine biologist. Though for the moment that dream seems to have stopped I still love snorkeling, walking on the beach, and hope to someday learn how to man a sailing vessle.

At the moment I would say I haven't found my work passion, but I try to not let that get to me. The positive takaway just means that I'm willing to try and do different things. But until I do find out what that is I get to spend my time with my highschool school sweet heart of 13 years. After this semester we plan to make the marriage official.
